## Project Overview
This Power BI project combines two interactive dashboards — Credit Card Customer Report and Credit Card Transaction Report — to deliver a complete overview of how customers use credit cards and how their transactions drive revenue.

It helps identify profitable customer segments, spending behavior, card performance, and income-based trends, providing deep insights for financial decision-making.

## Objective
To analyze customer demographics, transaction behavior, and revenue performance across various credit card categories and income groups, supporting business growth and customer targeting strategies.

## Dashboard Highlights
---Credit Card Customer Report
. Revenue and income distribution across job type, education level, income group, and age group.
. Businessmen and graduates lead in total revenue contribution.
. Customers aged 40–50 are the most profitable group.
. Interactive filters for quarter, gender, income group, and card type (Silver, Blue, Gold, Platinum).

---Credit Card Transaction Report
. Transaction breakdown by card category, payment method, and expenditure type.
. Blue card customers generate the highest revenue (46M).
. Entertainment and fuel are the top expenditure categories.
. Swipe transactions dominate with 35M in revenue.
. Quarter 2 recorded the highest transaction revenue (14.2M).
